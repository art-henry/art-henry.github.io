About Me and My Experience

Web developer from Odessa, Henrik Mkhitarian. I have experience working with HTML5, CSS3 (including SCSS and SASS), PHP, JavaScript, and Git. Worked with tools like VS Code, Kommo CRM, Figma, HeidiSQL, and FileZilla. I obtained my education at the National Economic University and took web development courses on Udemy, including "Modern JavaScript + Vue from Scratch on Real Projects".

I specialize in developing interactive websites, email integration through nodemailer, creating functional carts for e-commerce sites, and have experience with hosting platforms, particularly with Hostinger. My goal is to create attractive, fast websites that provide a memorable user experience.
